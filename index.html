About Me

As a seasoned Full-Stack Web Developer with over five years of professional experience, I bring a wealth of expertise to every project I undertake. Currently, I am engaged as a freelancer with Andela, based in the United States (Remote). My previous engagements include positions at distinguished tech firms such as Urhitech Ltd in Tema, Ghana, and CUBS in Russia (Remote), as well as within government organizations like GTEC. Additionally, I've collaborated with multiple tech startups spanning various sectors including FinTech, Healthcare, and E-Commerce. My skill set encompasses a comprehensive understanding of both Frontend and Backend development, along with proficiency in crafting REST APIs, architecting Microservices, implementing Distributed systems, leveraging Containerization, and orchestrating CI/CD pipelines. Furthermore, I possess adeptness in Cloud Technologies, particularly within the AWS ecosystem.

My journey into the tech realm began with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from the esteemed University of Cape Coast in Ghana. During breaks, I actively contributed to the development of software solutions for startups such as Jumeni Technology and AgroInnova, both of which were funded by MEST (Meltwater Entrepreneur School of Technology). This hands-on experience not only honed my technical skills but also instilled in me a deep appreciation for innovation and entrepreneurship. I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems and thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments. My diverse background equips me with a unique perspective and enables me to deliver robust, scalable solutions that drive business success.

This system takes away the hectic and manual way of post-retirement applications by lectures to the Ghana Tertiary education Commission (GTEC). In this system, the registrars office of the various universities creates an account for a lecturer who deserves a post-retirement. Lecturer then applies through the Registrar for approval or rejection. If Registrar approves, application moves to GTEC for final approval or rejection but if rejected by the registrar application does not move to GTEC.
